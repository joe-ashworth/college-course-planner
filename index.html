College Course Planner

Android Application: A D424 Capstone Project

This application was created to help students take control of their curriculum planning by providing the following functionalities:

  • Custom Term Creation: Students can easily create custom terms (Fall 2024, Spring 2025) within the app, defining the start and end dates for each term.
  • Flexible Course and Assessment Selection: Students can add courses to their chosen terms and assessments to their courses.
  • Search: Students can search for instances of relevant keywords within the courses that have been added to the app.
  • Notifications: The application incorporates reminder features to notify students of upcoming class start and end dates, assignment deadlines, and other important events.
